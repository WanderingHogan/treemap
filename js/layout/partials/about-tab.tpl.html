Built with:

Angular, leafletjs, bootstrap. Basemaps from MapBox.

Date from:

NOAA - Best Track Data (HURDAT2), which you can find here: NHC Data Archive, NOAA National Hurricane Center. Side note - the data comes in a pain in the butt format, and every time you load this page you are re-downloading and processing their terrible CSV into a usable format in your browser, which might eat a few megabytes of your data plan.
